BulkMess Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Last Updated: September 28, 2024 • Effective Date: September 28, 2024

📋 Privacy Summary

What You Should Know

  • Local Data Storage: Your contacts and messages are stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We don't upload your personal data to our servers
  • iOS Integration: Uses native iOS Messages and Contacts with your permission
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We don't share your data with advertisers or analytics companies
  • Optional Features: All data sharing features are opt-in and clearly explained

🏢 About This Policy

Who We Are

BulkMess is developed by Daniil Mukashev. This Privacy Policy describes how the BulkMess iOS application ("App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our SMS campaign management application.

This policy applies to:

  • The BulkMess iOS application
  • Any related services or features
  • Our support website and documentation

📱 Information We Collect

📞 Contact Information

Phone numbers, names, and other contact details you import or create within the app.

Storage: Local device only

💬 Message Content

Text templates and message content you create for your campaigns.

Storage: Local device only

📊 Usage Analytics

Basic app usage statistics to improve functionality (optional).

Storage: Anonymous and aggregated

🛠️ Technical Data

Device model, iOS version, and app version for support purposes.

Storage: Local logs only

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Your actual sent messages content
  • ❌ Recipients' personal information beyond phone numbers
  • ❌ Location data or device identifiers
  • ❌ Financial or payment information
  • ❌ Social media profiles or accounts
  • ❌ Browsing history or other app usage

🔒 How We Use Your Information

Primary Functions

  1. SMS Campaign Management: To send bulk messages using iOS Messages app
  2. Contact Organization: To manage and group your contacts
  3. Template Creation: To store and apply message templates
  4. Campaign Analytics: To show delivery status and campaign results
  5. App Functionality: To provide core features like scheduling and automation

Optional Features

  • iCloud Backup: If enabled by you, basic app settings may sync via iCloud
  • Shortcuts Integration: To create iOS Shortcuts for automated messaging
  • Error Reporting: Anonymous crash reports to improve app stability (opt-in)

🔐 Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

All your personal data (contacts, messages, templates) is stored locally on your device using iOS Core Data framework. This information:

  • ✅ Remains on your device at all times
  • ✅ Is protected by iOS security features
  • ✅ Is backed up with your device backups (if you choose)
  • ✅ Is deleted when you delete the app

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted using iOS standard encryption
  • Access Control: App requires your device authentication to access
  • No Remote Access: We cannot access your data remotely
  • Secure Transmission: Any optional data transmission uses HTTPS

📤 Data Sharing

⚠️ Important: We Do Not Sell Your Data

BulkMess does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data stays with you.

When Data May Be Shared

  1. iOS Messages Integration: Message content is shared with iOS Messages app to send SMS (this is how the app functions)
  2. iOS Contacts Integration: Contact information is accessed from your Contacts app (with your permission)
  3. iCloud Sync: Basic app preferences may sync via your iCloud account (if you enable this feature)
  4. Legal Requirements: Only if required by law and only the minimum necessary information

👤 Your Rights

Data Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modify: Edit or update any information
  • Delete: Remove specific data or clear all data
  • Export: Export your data in common formats
  • Portability: Move your data to other apps

Privacy Settings

You can control:

  • Contact access permissions
  • Optional analytics collection
  • iCloud sync preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Shortcuts integration

👶 Children's Privacy

Age Requirements

BulkMess is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

For users between 13-17 years old, parental consent may be required depending on your location's laws.

🌍 International Users

Global Compliance

BulkMess is designed to comply with privacy laws worldwide, including:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California, USA)
  • PIPEDA (Canada)
  • Privacy Act (Australia)

Since data is stored locally on your device, most international data transfer regulations don't apply. However, we maintain high privacy standards regardless of your location.

  2. Significant changes will be announced in the app
  3. You'll be notified of major changes that affect your data
  4. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy
